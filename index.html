ABOUT THE MEADOW

The Meadow is a new type of space. A clever, playful and stylish place for market research, creative workshops and individuals looking for a daytime workplace hub.

The Meadow brings together stunning Victorian architecture with an imaginative boutique fit-out as well as all the modern, practical touches and CBD convenience that you could ask for.

Working with some of Australia’s leading specialists in creating bespoke spaces and experiences, we’ve lovingly restored the first floor of this 1840’s original malt house. Inspired by the feeling of being surrounded by the natural world, we have repurposed the space to create Melbourne’s first premium market research rooms and workshop space.

We invite you to explore what is a breakthrough for research and workshop facilities in Melbourne. Our unique environment will enhance what you bring to your clients and their experience in working with you.
