SPACES  »  BOARDROOM

The most traditional of all of our spaces, the boardroom is framed by the beautiful original brick archways of our Victorian space and towering tree filled windows. The design of the space takes inspiration from a naturalist's study, drawing on themes of exploration and discovery.

Perfect for

  • - Traditional qualitative market research (groups and in-depths)
  • - Corporate meetings and workshops.

Capacity

10 + moderator

Function

Viewing room windows can be entirely closed off or left open depending on your requirements.

Utilities

  • - High Definition recording
  • - In-room LCD viewing facilities (from DVD and Laptop)
  • - Stimulus ledges
  • - Whiteboard and butchers paper easels

3.5 hours - $635

6 hours - $815
Day hire (8.30 – 17.30) - $800

Recording, hosts, tea and coffee, refreshments and snacks.
